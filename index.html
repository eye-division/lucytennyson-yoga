Yoga classes

Looking ahead, studio classes will resume at St Ethelwolds on Monday 14th June, with a new afternoon class at 2pm, focussing on movement and flexibility and welcomes beginners. The popular Wednesday evening classes return on the 16th at 5.15pm and 7pm.  Zoom will continue on Mondays at 6pm, and the gentle/stretch class will move to Tuesdays 5-6pm

I hope to start outdoor classes from May when the weather allows.
