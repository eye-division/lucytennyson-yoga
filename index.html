ONLINE YOGA

I am now teaching three classes a week online using the Zoom application. It's easy to use and if you'd like to try a class is get in touch.  I'll send you a link back by email inviting you to join. If you are a new student to me, please use the contact form on this website, and I can arrange to have a chat with you and send a short joining form with questions about your health.

My classes

Normally, I teach in Abingdon and Oxford. My classes are open to all, from beginners to more experienced students, and taught in a relatively slow 'hatha' style - beginners are always welcome. If y0u have specific health issues, I am also able to offer Yoga Therapy sessions (one to one) in my home studio in north Oxford (or your home by arrangement).

The levels below are just a guide, as all of my classes are taken at a relatively slow pace.

My philosophy is you don't need to be bendy to do yoga! In fact, quite the opposite, it's the stiff and creaky among us who stand to benefit the most. I strongly believe that yoga should be for everyone, of all ages and all levels of fitness. I teach in a gentle hatha style that is ideal for beginners, sports people with tight muscles, and older students, as well as more experienced students seeking a deeper practice.
