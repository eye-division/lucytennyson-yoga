Yoga classes

My teaching term restarts on 7th September, and I am offering a choice of online classes using  Zoom, or indoor studio classes with limited numbers run according to Government regulations. All are welcome especially beginners and those looking for good stretch and chance to relax after work. I teach in a relatively gentle 'hatha' style to suit all levels of fitness. If y0u have specific health issues, I am also able to offer Yoga Therapy sessions (one to one).

Beginners

If you are a complete beginner or haven't done much recently, and like to try a Zoom class just get in touch. I will then arrange a chat on the phone and/or a one to one tutorial to get you started.
