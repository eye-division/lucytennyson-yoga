Yoga classes

I teach in a relatively gentle 'hatha' style to suit all ages and levels of fitness, and welcome beginners. I teach both in the studio (from September ) and online using Zoom. During August, I am teaching as follows:  Monday 2pm outdoors in the garden at St Ethelwolds in Abingdon, and 6pm on Zoom; and on  Wednesdays outdoors at St Ethelwolds at 5.30pm 

I will return to the studio in September at St Ethelwolds in Abingdon on Mondays at 2pm,  and Wednesdays at 5.15pm and 7pm. During September I will be launching a new slow and gentle class on Tuesday mornings, focussing on movement and flexibility.  I will continue to teach online using Zoom on Mondays at 6pm (all levels).
