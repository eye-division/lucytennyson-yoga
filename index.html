ABINGDON YOGA : Gentle and accessible yoga for all levels, all ages! 

I teach in a softer 'hatha' style to suit all ages and levels of fitness, and welcome beginners. I have been teaching yoga in Abingdon for 12 years, and more recently online using Zoom. 

 

We practise in the beautiful garden at St Ethelwolds in Abingdon in the summer months.

Get in touch using the contact page on this website.
