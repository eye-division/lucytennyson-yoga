ABINGDON YOGA  AND ONLINE ZOOM 

Gentle and accessible yoga for all levels, all ages! 

I teach in a softer 'hatha' style to suit all ages and levels of fitness, and welcome beginners. I have been teaching yoga in Abingdon for 15 years, and more recently online using Zoom.  My studio based classes are often full, as limited to eight spaces, so do get in touch to book your place.
