For information about yoga classes in Abingdon and on Zoom, read on ....

I teach in a relatively gentle 'hatha' style to suit all ages and levels of fitness, and welcome beginners. I have been teaching yoga in Abingdon for 12 years, and more recently online using Zoom. 

ABINGDON YOGA 

This summer we practise in the beautiful setting of the garden of the St Ethelwolds retreat centre in central Abingdon: Every Monday at 2 pm and Wednesday at 6 pm from 27 July until September (drop in welcome)  Come and join us!

If you have any questions you can get in touch using the contact page on this website.

The following timetable will resume from September:

Monday 2-3.15pm Afternoon yoga (all levels)in the studio at St Ethelwolds (outdoors if fine)

6-7PM Hatha flow (all welcome) online Zoom.

Wednesday   two evenings classes in the Sanctuary, St Ethelwolds 5.15 - 6.30 pm;
and  7-8.15 pm
