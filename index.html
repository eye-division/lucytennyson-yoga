Yoga classes

I teach in Abingdon and Oxford. On Mondays, I have classes in Abingdon at 12pm and 7.30pm, and in Radley at 10.15am; on Tuesdays I offer a class to University staff at 6pm; on Wednesdays I teach two classes in Abingdon, at 3.45pm and 5.45pm. See the classes page for more info, and use the contact form on this website to get in touch.

Please give me a call, or use the contact page on this site, to find out more. Timetable

I strongly believe that yoga is for everyone, and teach in a gentle hatha style that is ideal for beginners, sports people and older students, as well as more experienced students seeking a deeper practice. Read more about yoga here.

You are welcome to join any of my classes on a drop-in basis, but it is always helpful to drop me an email beforehand. Drop in costs £9 or £10 depending on the venue, but I offer a 'pass' which works out at around £8 per class, bookable in advance.

There are always spaces in Abingdon at Saturn and Larkmead School on Mondays as I have a large teaching space, and usually at my Oxford classes.

NEW! Gentle chair based yoga class at Radley on Mondays 10.15am-11.15am

(Please note - class cancelled on Monday 6th as heating has broken down)

It's never too late to start! This class is an ideal way of getting started if you are older, very stiff, or have trouble walking or standing for long. You'll find that it's possible to do a seated version of many of the main yoga poses so there will be plenty to do. We include standing and balancing movements during the class. Please get in touch if you are interested, or know anyone who might benefit.
